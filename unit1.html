Cashing a Check

The Process:

First thing to know is to always check with your bank first before doing anything. Having a good understanding of your banks rules can be very informative.

Here I will list out exactly what you will need to do in order to be sucessful when you take a trip to the bank. Getting in and out is key and knowing what you need to do before hand can make things go a lot faster

  1. Make sure it is your name on the pay to the order of line
  2. Make sure the correct money amount is wrote on the line
  3. Make sure you have enough funds if the check is not off of your bank
  4. Do not try to cash a check that is not made out to you
  5. Make sure you sign the back of your check before cashing it

Basic Skills

  • Be kind to tellers
  • Always have a form of id
  • Say thank you when leaving
  • Count your money back before leaving
